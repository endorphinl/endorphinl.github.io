BRUCE keynote

by hokjoung on 2014
- BRUCE has three components. 1. Parser 2. Loader 3. Renderer
- the parser is the part of BRUCE that deals with the queries our users submit.
- the loader is in charge of loading the right data sets into the system
- the renderer will then render the knowledge panel with the data
- now, the most important part of BRUCE is its capability to understand the search queries. BRUCE can understand the meanings in the texts. I would like to invite up our very own YJ to explain the parser.
- yj explains how the parser works
- thank you
- hk explains how the loader works
- hk explains how the renderer works
- hk explains bruce.think and bruce.draw - think is just loading the data.
- think is there for multi purposes. we want to be able to dynamically change our panels. Our mobile web might have a different panel design from the desktop version.
- so we want to be able to isolate just the data so we can customize.
- but most importantly, our main means of communication with our mobile app is JSON, hence, 'think' is a perfect way to communicate.
