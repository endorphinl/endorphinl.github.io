Tis a voice

by hokjoung on 2014

Lights of pink and purple. As if to form a rift from the indisputable owner/host of the sky. tis a voice of their rebellion against darkness for they wish to deny that they may fall in despair scrutinizing/contemplating their lonliness in the world. (because they cant accept that they can be depressed by their loneliness in the world). Tis a gesture to indicate we are still here in the vast cosmic arena to the wondering observer perhaps...
