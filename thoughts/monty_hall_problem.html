Monty hall problem

by hokjoung on 2014

To explain why it is not 50/50 I will give examples. Imagine 3 doors. The car is behind the first one:

1) You choose 1st door. Monty opens 2nd. You switch and get a zonk. You lose
2) You choose 2nd door. Monty now must open 3rd door. You switch. You win a car.
3) You choose 3rd door. Monty must open 2nd door. You switch and win a car.
2/3 you win by switching
